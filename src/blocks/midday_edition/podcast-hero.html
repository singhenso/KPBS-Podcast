KPBS Midday Edition

KPBS Midday Edition is a daily radio news magazine and podcast that keeps San Diego in the know on everything from politics to the arts. Midday Edition airs weekdays from noon to 1 p.m. and again from 8 to 9 p.m. You can also find in it your favorite podcast app.
